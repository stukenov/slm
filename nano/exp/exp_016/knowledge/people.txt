Abay Kunanbayev is a Kazakh poet, philosopher, composer, and founder of modern Kazakh written literature.
Mukhtar Auezov is a Kazakh writer, playwright, and scholar, author of the novel "The Path of Abai".
Al-Farabi is a medieval philosopher, scientist, and music theorist from Kazakhstan, known as the "Second Teacher" after Aristotle.
Toktar Aubakirov is the first Kazakh cosmonaut who flew to space in 1991 aboard Soyuz TM-13.
Dinmukhamed Kunayev was a Soviet-era leader of the Kazakh SSR and a member of the Politburo.
Nursultan Nazarbayev is the first President of independent Kazakhstan who served from 1991 to 2019.
Kassym-Jomart Tokayev is the current President of Kazakhstan since 2019.
