Astana is the capital of Kazakhstan since 1997, previously known as Akmola and Nur-Sultan.
Almaty is the largest city in Kazakhstan and was its capital until 1997.
Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world and the ninth largest country overall.
Lake Balkhash is one of the largest lakes in Asia, located in southeastern Kazakhstan.
The Caspian Sea borders western Kazakhstan and is the largest enclosed inland body of water on Earth.
Baikonur Cosmodrome is the world's first and largest operational space launch facility, located in Kazakhstan.
