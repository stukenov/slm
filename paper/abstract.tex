\begin{abstract}
We present \sozkz{}, a family of small language models trained from scratch
exclusively on Kazakh text, spanning four parameter scales from 50M to 600M.
We demonstrate that dedicated monolingual models, paired with a
purpose-built tokenizer, can match or exceed much larger multilingual
models on core Kazakh NLP tasks while requiring orders-of-magnitude
fewer parameters.  We evaluate all models on a suite of six benchmarks
and release the full model family, tokenizer, and training pipeline
to the community.
\end{abstract}
