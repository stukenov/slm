## Notes

- ELDP provides grants for documentation of endangered languages worldwide.
- Official grant page says there are no restrictions on nationality of PI or host institution.
- Main annual round is open from mid-June to 1 October each year.
- Rapid Grants are rolling, up to EUR 5,000, and do not require a host institution.
- This is not a pure model-training grant, but it could strongly fit corpus building, documentation, archiving, and language-resource creation around Kazakh varieties, dialects, or oral data.
- Grants overview: https://grants.eldp.net/en
- Main site: https://www.eldp.net/
- Rapid Grants details: https://www.eldp.net/en/our%2Bgrants/rapid%2Bgrants/
