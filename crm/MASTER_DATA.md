## Ключевые факты для писем

- **Tokenizer**: Custom 32K Kazakh BPE tokenizer, 38% better compression than multilingual alternatives
- **Architecture**: Llama (standard HuggingFace-compatible)
- **Training data**: 840M tokens Kazakh text (cleaned, deduplicated)
- **Unique value**: First open-source family of native Kazakh LLMs
- **Impact**: Kazakh is spoken by ~15M people, severely underrepresented in NLP
- **All models**: MIT license, open weights, open tokenizer
